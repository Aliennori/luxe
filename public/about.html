About us

Welcome to Luxe Hotel

Discover a world of refined elegance and impeccable hospitality at Luxe Hotel. Nestled in the heart of Westlands, our luxurious haven stands as a testament to the art of indulgence. From the moment you step inside, you will be enveloped in an ambiance of opulence and sophistication, where every detail has been meticulously curated to ensure your stay transcends all expectations.When you think about Luxe Hotel, we hope unparalleled luxury comes to mind. But what is luxury, really? Our answer may surprise you.

To us, true luxury is a meaningful sense of belonging. It is a dedicated focus on how people want to be treated, grounded in the genuine care you experience during your stay and defined by an abundance of humanity and generosity. This starts with our passionate team, welcoming you to be the centre of our world, anywhere in the world â€“ and always with a distinctly human touch.

Our Story

Luxe Hotel was born out of a vision to create an oasis of tranquility and grandeur within the bustling heart of Nairobi. With a rich history spanning over 50 years, our establishment has evolved into a symbol of prestige and excellence. From its inception, Luxe Hotel has played host to discerning travelers, celebrities, and dignitaries from around the world, earning a reputation as a timeless sanctuary that caters to the most refined tastes.

Culture and Traditions

We recognize our responsibility as guardians of the local heritage. Luxe actively engages in community initiatives and supports organizations dedicated to preserving cultural landmarks, traditional craftsmanship, and indigenous knowledge. When you stay with us, you contribute to these meaningful endeavors, ensuring that the essence of Nairobi continues to thrive.
